Guide & Best Practices
How to protect your data
Where is my data stored?
maise stores everything directly in your browser — think of it like a notebook that lives inside Chrome or Safari on your specific device. There is no account, no cloud, and no server.
⚠ Important
Your data can be permanently lost if you clear your browser history or cookies, switch browsers, open on a new device, or reinstall your browser. Think of it like keeping cash in a drawer — if the drawer disappears, so does the cash.
How to back up your data
1
Tap ⚙ Setup in the top right menu.
2
Tap Export JSON in the Data section.
3
Save the file to Google Drive, iCloud, or email it to yourself.
How to restore your data
1
Open maise in your browser.
2
Tap ⚙ Setup → Import JSON.
3
Select your backup file. Everything comes back instantly.
Multiple devices
maise does not sync automatically. Export on one device, transfer the file (email, AirDrop, Google Drive), and import on the other.

cash flow calendar
Welcome! maise is a simple, private cash flow calendar that helps you track what's coming in and going out — day by day, month by month.
Your data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to a server. You're always in control.
To get started, add your first account. Then you can add transactions manually or import directly from your bank.
