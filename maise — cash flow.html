Guide & Best Practices
How maise works and how to protect your data
Where is my data stored?
maise stores all of your data directly in your browser — think of it like a notebook that lives inside Chrome or Safari on your specific device. There is no account, no cloud, and no server storing your information.
⚠ Important to understand
Because your data lives in your browser, it can be permanently lost if you clear your browser history or cookies, switch to a different browser, open the app on a different device, or uninstall and reinstall your browser. Think of it like keeping your budget in a notebook — if the notebook gets thrown away, the data is gone.
The good news: you are in full control. By exporting a backup file regularly, you can always restore your data.
How to back up your data
maise lets you export all of your data as a single file. Do this regularly — we recommend at least once a week.
1
Tap ⚙ Setup in the top right corner of the app.
2
Scroll down to the Data section at the bottom of Setup.
3
Tap Export JSON. A file called maise-backup.json will download to your device.
4
Save this file somewhere safe — your Downloads folder, iCloud Drive, Google Drive, or email it to yourself.
How to restore your data
If you switch devices, clear your browser, or open maise somewhere new, here is how to get your data back:
1
Open maise in your browser.
2
Tap ⚙ Setup → Import JSON.
3
Select your maise-backup.json file. Everything — your accounts, transactions, recurring rules, and settings — will be restored instantly.
Using maise on multiple devices
maise does not automatically sync between devices. If you use it on both your phone and your computer, you will need to manually transfer your data using the Export and Import steps above. Export on one device, transfer the file (email, AirDrop, Google Drive, etc.), and import on the other.
Quick tips
✓
Export your backup at least once a week — maise will remind you.
✓
Keep a copy of your backup file in two places (e.g. your device and Google Drive).
✓
Export before switching to a new version of the app — then re-import to pick up right where you left off.
✓
Do not clear your browser history/cookies if you want to keep your data between exports.
Weekly Reminder
Back up your data
It has been 7 days since your last backup. maise keeps all your data in your browser — not in the cloud. That means if you clear your browser, switch devices, or something goes wrong, your data is gone unless you have a backup file.
Exporting takes about 5 seconds. We strongly recommend doing it at least once a week.
